A New York success story.

Arc90 needed a makeover after over half a decade in New York City. The challenge was to showcase a wide portfolio of work to illustrate the shop’s design and technical prowess. The result is a compelling narrative that speaks to the firm’s biggest asset — its people.

  • Creative Lead

Passion fuels everything.

Every line of code is a committment to quality and a love of their craft. Because of this drive, the new Arc90.com was as much an expression of the projects as it was a reflection of the people who created them. Attention to detail can be found throughout the website in honor of the Arc90 tradition.

  • Engaging Layouts

    Organizing images and copy to create an inviting layout was critical to the narrative for each project.

  • Vibrant Colors

    Implementing themes helped give each story a unique character. Color helped draw the eye through the narrative as a user scrolled.

  • Beautiful Typography

    The big, punchy headlines that compliment each layout feature fonts from Hoefler & Co., including the gorgeous Gotham.

People Power

People make Arc90. To drive home this point, each project narrative ended by revealing the men and women behind the project, literally. It was a clever way to showcase such a talented group of people and instill a bit of charm to each page.
