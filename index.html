Reminders that work

01

Background

Doo is an app I conceptualized, designed, and built as a team of one. I created it in response to the frustration I felt trying to find a simple, elegant to-do app. Popular solutions were either too complicated or too underwhelming. Doo has a deliberate, streamlined feature set rooted in psychology and stress reduction. This means that instead of replicating a pencil and paper, Doo helps you set achievable goals, and gives you the means to become a more disciplined you.

02

The Stack

A more productive you, one card at a time

The stack makes Doo special. Instead of showing tasks in a list, Doo shows one task at a time to help you focus. Swipe cards down to get reminded later or up to mark them completed.

  • Complete
  • Snooze

Works with your brain

Procrastination is easy without an obvious first step. Doo is designed as a natural extension of how your brain works by helping you keep things simple and achievable.

03

Task Creation

Taking convention further

The navigation pattern in Doo builds on the iOS Human Interface Guidelines to reinforce the card metaphor. Each view’s hierarchy preserves the placement of common actions while allowing the content to come forward. And cards enter and exit with momentum and spring. The result is a familiar, more delightful experience.

04

The List

Prepare for the day

Get a jump on upcoming tasks or search for an existing reminder. Sort to-dos in place so there’s never ambiguity.

05

The Doo Way To Work

  • Break down projects

    Make sure tasks have a single, achievable step.

  • Select a few tasks

    Pick up to three tasks and snooze the rest. Optionally sort tasks by priority.

  • Get things done

    Complete your selected tasks one-by-one. See progress day by day.

06

Illustrations

A productivity app with warmth

Inviting illustrations add personality in ways a flat list never could. This makes using Doo a more satisfying experience. And each image is chosen automatically based on the task title. Manage your free time, not your to-dos.

07

Reception

 
I absolutely love this app... *incredibly* satisfying. — Buzzfeed

About a week after launching on Product Hunt, Doo was featured by Apple twice. The first in Best New Apps and the second in the Best of February. The app got attention from sites big and small, including Wired and Buzzfeed.

I’m incredibly grateful to my friends and family for all of their support and encouragement. It was one of the most challenging periods of my life. I couldn’t be more excited to continue making Doo better and better.
