About me

I love designing and building products. When I’m finally able to elegantly solve a problem, there’s nothing better. I see my work as an extension of myself, and so each day is an opportunity to learn something new, to challenge myself, and to try and show someone else what I’ve learned.

When I'm not working, you can probably find me up past midnight toiling away on a side project for one of the many causes that are near and dear to my heart. My most recent side project was NoWayNSA.com. Civil and social rights are pretty big on my list of favorite things.

It was designed and built in a few days so it’s got some rough edges, but I truly believe in the power of the web. Even if I’m only able to inform one person, it was worth it. The Web is a wonderful place.

Outside the tech world, I’m into weightlifting, skiing, and photography. Waking up in the morning to try and break a personal record is one of my favorite things. It’s also fun to teach, so I’m always inviting others to join. For more thoughts (and the occasional rant), you can find me on Twitter and Instagram.
