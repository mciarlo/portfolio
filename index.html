About Mike

Welcome to my little piece of the Internet. I’m an interidsciplinary designer and developer living in New York City. A piece of me goes into every product I create. I love seeing the "wow" moment on people’s faces. I’m consistently pushing those around me to push themsevles.

When I'm not designing and building products, I love to ski, lift weights, and explore the city with my camera. I'm also involved in many activist movements, including net neutrality, the NSA scandal, and global warming.
