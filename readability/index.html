Readability

Made for reading.

Readability was a unique and rewarding challenge to design and build from scratch. The beautiful typography and unique user interactions helped secure its spot in the App Store as one of the most beautiful reading platforms.

  • Creative Lead
  • iOS Developer

Thoughtful details.

The challenge I had was to design a gorgeous user interface without sacrificing any core features. The result is an application that delights at every chance. Subtle animations bring the interface to life. Beautiful typography makes the reading experience a pleasure. And technology improvements allow visually impaired readers a whole new level of accessibility.

  • Support for VoiceOver and iOS accessibility features.

  • Always up-to-date with background refresh.

  • Remembers where you were with app restoration.

Readabilicons

Images increase an app’s size and that means higher data usage and longer download times. The solution was a custom icon set I created exclusively for the app. Readability’s application size is markedly less than competitors thanks to the elimination of images.

Pretty faces.

Readability is first and foremost a reading platform, so great typography had to be the number one feature. I made hundreds of text adjustments to maximize legibility and comprehension across devices and orientations. The largest font size got larger to accomodate readers of all ages. And of course, the typefaces from Hoefler & Co. look gorgeous on the Retina Display.

Fun & Familiar.

Readability feels like a treasured novel — familiar and comforting. I designed each animation to add charm and each interaction to help readers connect to their content. The gestures feel so natural that I observed people (myself included) attempting to use them in other apps. Now that’s something.
