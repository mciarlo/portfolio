Readability

Made for reading.

Designed and built from scratch, the new Readability was a pleasure to design and build. The typography and user interactions help secure it’s spot in the App Store as the world’s most beautiful reading platform.

Thoughtful details.

The challenge for Readability was to build a gorgeous user interface without sacrificing any core features users expect from the service. The result is an application that delights at every chance. Subtle animations bring the interface to life. Beautiful typography makes the reading experience a pleasure. And technology improvements allow visually impaired readers a whole new level of accessibility.

  • Support for VoiceOver and iOS accessibility features.

  • Always up-to-date with background refresh.

  • Remembers where you were with app restoration.

Readabilicons

Images can make an application size bloat and that means more data usage and longer download times. The solution? Readabilicons: a custom icon set that feels right at home on iOS 7. Readability’s application size is markedly less than competitors thanks to the elimination of all images.

    • Refined Typography.

    Readability places the most emphasis on typography of all the reading services, and there's a reason for it: typography matters. Two font families were cut. Hundreds of adjustments were made to maximize legibility and comprehension across devices and orientations. The largest font size got larger to accomodate readers of all ages. Fonts from Hoefler & Co. elevate the reading experience into something like a printed page.

    Fun & Familiar.

    The interactions within Readability delight and surprise users. Everything is designed to make reading easy and comfortable. Everything feels familiar — there's nothing out of place. Gestures become a natural part of a users workflow while subtle animations create a smooth experience even months after first install.
